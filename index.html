Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FoundIt (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our Flutter mobile application.

Data we collect
  • Full name — to personalise your account and lost item reports.
  • Email address — for account verification, password recovery, and service updates.
  • Phone number — to enable direct contact between finders and owners, and for two-factor authentication.

How we use your data:

  • Create and manage your FoundIt account.
  • Match lost & found items and facilitate secure communication.
  • Send important notifications (e.g., item matches, privacy updates).
  • Improve app performance and safety (anonymised analytics).
Data protection: We use encryption (TLS) and secure storage. Your phone number is only visible to other users when you explicitly respond to a found item.
Confidentiality

FoundIt treats your personal information as confidential. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Internal access: Only authorised personnel with a need-to-know basis can access personal data (e.g., support team).
  • Third-party sharing: We share data only with trusted service providers (cloud hosting, push notifications) that comply with strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Anonymisation: Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be used for research and analytics.
We enforce confidentiality by contract and technical measures. Any breach is subject to investigation and legal action.
